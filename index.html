Maple Pancakes

There are few things that compare to the taste of some good thick pancakes with maple sirup straight from the great north, Canada.

The recipe you're about to read should give you pancakes for about 4 people. Your milage may vary.
Now to get started cooking these pancakes you're going to need a few things:

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 4.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 3-4 Eggs, large
  • 2.25 cups 2% milk
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 3 tbs maple sirup
  • Optional: butter and bacon for topping later


Once you have all the ingredients we can get started with the cooking.

Now let's get started:

  1. Get a large bowl. Now put your dry ingredients in and give them a rough mix.
  2. While mixing add eggs and milk, then the oil and syrup.
  3. Continue mixing until its all blended together with no lumps.
  4. Get a pan heated on medium. Once the pan is hot use a ladle to pour in the batter.
  5. Flip once the bubbles start comming through to the top and the bottom is golden brown.
  6. Serve with maple sirup and a bit of butter.
Remember to only use Canadian maple sirup or the mounties will come for you.
Tip: Add bacon on the side, or don't, I'm a recipe not a cop.
